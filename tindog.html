The first social app for dog lovers who want to connect with other owners nearby that share a similar passion for canines. Whether it's to build new friendships or make a love connection. Tindog is a fun way to find the pawfect match with a little help from our furry friends.

Based on GPS location, Tindog will suggest nearby matches to help socialize your pet. Dogs are pack animals so it's equally as important to help them meet other dogs in the area. By matching with neighborhood dogs your furry friend will never be alone again.
